Geekbench hasn't updated their Mac benchmarks page to include the 2020 13-inch MacBook Pro yet, so I put something together to grab that myself. What you see below is generated by averaging the search results from Geekbench[^results] for the CPU models mentioned [here](https://www.notebookcheck.net/Geekbench-confirms-new-MacBook-Pro-model-identifiers-and-Core-i5-1038NG7-processor.464225.0.html).

In summary, with 1,034 results so far for the i5 and 558 for the i7, the i7 is only outperforming the i5 by 6-7%[^update]. I’m not sure that’s worth the $200 extra price tag.
